FAQ

What does “non-invasive” mean?

No dismantling or disassembly. The inspection is visual and operational only, based on what’s observable at the time.

Do you road test every vehicle?

Where permitted and safe. If a road test can’t be completed, it is noted and conclusions are limited accordingly.

Can you guarantee there are no hidden issues?

No. Hidden or intermittent faults can exist. The report reflects observable condition at the time of inspection.

What does scan tool diagnostics include?

Premium includes reading fault codes where the vehicle supports it. Results are recorded in the report.
