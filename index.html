FAQ

Do you road test every vehicle?

Yes where permitted and safe. If a road test can’t be completed, it will be noted in the report and conclusions will be limited accordingly.

Do you dismantle anything?

No. Inspections are non-invasive visual and operational checks only. No wheel removal or component disassembly.

Can you guarantee there are no hidden issues?

No. Hidden or intermittent faults can exist. The report reflects observable condition at the time of inspection.

Do you scan for fault codes?

Scan tool diagnostics are included in Premium (where supported by the vehicle and diagnostic port access is available).

How fast do I receive the report?

Typically same day. Timing depends on schedule and inspection conditions.

What suburbs do you cover?

Eastern Suburbs Sydney as standard (Bondi, Randwick, Coogee, Maroubra, Paddington, Rose Bay, Double Bay). Sydney metro is available by quote.
