FAQ

Can we attend the inspection?

Yes. Standard and Deluxe include the option to attend for a quick start/end walkthrough (where access is permitted at the location).

Do you deal with the seller/dealer?

Yes. We coordinate access and timing directly with the seller or dealership — you don’t have to chase anyone.

What is a PPSR check?

A PPSR check helps identify if a vehicle is recorded as having finance owing and may show stolen or written-off records. Included in Deluxe.

How fast do we get the report?

Same-day in most cases, depending on access and scheduling. If timing is tight, we’ll tell you up front.

What does “non-invasive” mean?

No dismantling or disassembly. The inspection is visual and operational only, based on what’s observable at the time.

What vehicles do you inspect?

Cars, 4WDs, vans and light commercial vehicles. We don’t inspect heavy trucks or vehicles requiring a different licence class.

What if the seller won’t allow a road test?

We’ll note it in the report. If a check can’t be completed due to access/time/safety, conclusions are limited accordingly.

Is the deposit refundable?

The $50 deposit is refundable if you cancel more than 24 hours before the booking time. Cancellations within 24 hours may forfeit the deposit due to scheduling.
